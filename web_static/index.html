About us

Welcome to shielded , where we are dedicated to protecting girls against Female Genital Mutilation(FGM) and Sexual Gender-Based Violence(SGBV). Our mission is to create a safe and empowering world for girls, advocating their rights and well-being.

Our website provides resources and support to combat FGM, promote healthier rites of passage, and raise awareness about SGBV.

Through education, empowerment, and community engagement, we aim to challenge harmful traditions, foster gender equality, and advocate for justice and prevention. Access our resources, learn about global initiatives, and join us in the fight against FGM and SGBV to create a safer and more equitable world for all girls.

What We Do

Support for Survivors: We create a safe and supportive environment for FGM and SGBV survivors. Our website offers resources,helpline,services and information on support networks. We ensure survivors have access to comprehensive care, including medical, legal and psychosocial support, empowering their recovery.

Empowering Communities: We foster dialogue, challenge cultural norms, and engage with communities, parents, religious leaders, and local stakeholders. Through community participation, we promote gender equality, shift attitudes, and build support networks to protect girls from FGM and SGBV.

Provide coaching, mentorship, client-centered care and survivor-focused services for Gender-Based Violence (GBV). Our approach ensures that health workers prioritize and respect the individual needs, preferences,and priorities of each client.
